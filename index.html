About Me

Background

With over 8 years of experience in mechanical engineering, I specialize in product development, thermal analysis, and design optimization. I hold a Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering from MIT and a Bachelor's degree from UC Berkeley. Throughout my career, I've led cross-functional teams to deliver innovative solutions in automotive, aerospace, and consumer products industries.

Engineering Philosophy

I believe in data-driven design decisions combined with creative problem-solving. My approach centers on understanding the root cause of engineering challenges, leveraging simulation and prototyping to validate concepts, and iterating rapidly to achieve optimal solutions. Every project is an opportunity to push the boundaries of what's possible while maintaining practical manufacturability and cost-effectiveness.
